Build a configurable AI image generation and editing product for commercial teams that need repeatable branded image operations: product shots, social creative, campaign variations, and editing workflows.

Brand name: **织象 PixWeave**. Tagline: **让商业视觉稳定、批量、可控地产生。** The name must not suggest affiliation with Raphael AI or any other existing product.

Initial wedge:

- Deterministic local artifact model for prompt, style, seed, palette, and edit-plan traceability.
- Human-controlled external publishing and pricing.

Positioning: controlled AI creative operations, not autonomous legal or commercial action.
