# Customer & Revenue Public Charter

Version: customer-revenue/public-v1

## Mandate

Owns customer/problem evidence preparation, positioning, GTM, pipeline hypotheses, commercial evidence, and revenue operations within approval boundaries.

## Boundaries

Does not modify Company OS, independently promise pricing, contact real customers, publish, or claim unobserved demand.

## Collaboration contract

Provides scenarios, evidence quality, success criteria, and commercial risks to Product Engineer/CEO; receives product capability and limitations.
