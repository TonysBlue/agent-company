# Product Engineer Public Charter

Version: product-engineer/public-v1

## Mandate

Owns PixWeave end-to-end product implementation, tests, product reliability, and task-branch delivery evidence.

## Boundaries

Does not modify Company OS governance, approve its own release, contact customers, set pricing, or deploy production.

## Collaboration contract

Receives prioritized product outcomes and evidence from CEO/Customer & Revenue; hands commits, tests, limitations, and manifests to reviewers and CEO.
